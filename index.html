Maria Pavlenko

Biography

Hi, I’m Maria. I’m a User Experience Researcher from Moscow. I currently work as a User Experience Researcher Consultant. Also, I start learning Data Analysis to use as much user data as possible to create better user experience.

I created this webpage as a place to document everything I learned while as User Experience Researcher.

You can read my articles and information about me on this page. Have fun!

Experience Timeline

2010,
I started learning BSc Marketing Research in The St. Petersburg State University of Economics. Exceptional and my favorite modules: Statistics, Marketing analysis, Consumer behaviours, Methodology and Methods of Research.
2015,
During my studies, I had an internship as a Research Specialist in John Deere. Conducting quantitative studies of the presence of John Deere forestry and road construction equipment on the market of the Leningrad Region and the Republic of Karelia.
With a team of classmates, we won in the business game “Smart University”, from PETER-SERVICE Company a service dedicated to the development and marketing justification of a new software product for the university.
I was in an internship as a Marketing Analyst at digital agency ENDY, developed a project for Bank of St. Petersburg. Identificated of priority areas for the development of the internal portal for employees the Bank of St. Petersburg.
2016,
Worked at 2Nova Interactive Agency. Participated in accompanying and adapting the international e-commerce project for the Russian market.
Participated as an attracted by UX analyst expert in the Prian.ru project. Based on the surveys of users of the Prian.ru portal, the redesign of the personal cabinets of the portal users (individuals and corporate).
2017,
Worked at 1Point as a UX Researcher. Conducting qualitative research on user behavior. Development of methodologies for usability testing, selection of research methods for project tasks. Conducting laboratory and field studies with products users. Participation in projects: Sberbank-Technology, SQVR.ru, Nerf, Play-Doh, CIS Russia International School.
As an employee of 1Point through a staffing agency, worked under a contract on projects of IBM iX Russia.
2018,
Worked as a consultant in the field of User Experience Researcher on the IBM iX. Participation in projects in the banking sector and the oil and gas industries. Conduct user research, introduce UX research into product development.
2019,
At the moment I am learning Python and in the future, I want to explore but also develop user interfaces used more data about users.
You can learn more about my professional self on LinkedIn.
